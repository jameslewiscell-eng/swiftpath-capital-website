Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SwiftPath Capital Corporation (“SwiftPath Capital,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our websites, communicate with us, or apply for financing. Some data we process may be subject to federal financial privacy laws (e.g., the Gramm‑Leach‑Bliley Act, “GLBA”). Where GLBA applies, certain state‑law rights described below may not apply to that data.

1) Information We Collect

  • Identifiers (name, email, phone, IP address, device identifiers).
  • Commercial & application data (loan purpose, property address, entity info, budget, draw schedules).
  • Financial information (income ranges, bank statements, credit authorization results where permitted).
  • Property information (leases, rent roll, mortgage statements, tax and insurance details).
  • Internet activity (pages viewed, links clicked, referrers, analytics, and advertising interactions).
  • Communications (emails, calls, messages).

2) Sources

We collect information directly from you; automatically from your browser or device; from service providers (e.g., analytics, CRM, form tools); and from third parties you authorize (e.g., credit or verification vendors).

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and improve our services and websites.
  • Process applications, determine eligibility, underwrite, and service loans.
  • Communicate with you, including marketing (you can opt out at any time).
  • Detect, investigate, and help prevent fraud, security incidents, and misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4) Sharing

We share information with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, CRM, form processors, identity/credit verification) and lending partners in order to process your requests. We may also share as required by law or to protect rights, safety, and property.

5) Cookies, Analytics & Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality, analytics, and advertising. Partners may include Google Analytics/Ads (including remarketing) and HubSpot. You can control cookies in your browser and opt out of interest‑based ads via:

  • Google Ads Settings and AdChoices/Network Advertising Initiative opt‑out tools.
  • Google Analytics Opt‑out Browser Add‑on.

6) Data Retention

We retain information as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When no longer required, we delete or de‑identify information subject to our retention policies.

7) Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please use caution when sharing sensitive information online.

8) Your State Privacy Rights

Depending on your state (e.g., CA, CO, CT, UT, VA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information; to opt out of targeted advertising or “sales/sharing”; and to limit the use/disclosure of sensitive personal information. These rights may not apply to GLBA‑regulated data. To exercise rights or appeal a decision, contact us at privacy@swiftpathcapital.com or mail the address below.

9) Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

10) Do Not Track

We currently do not respond to browser “Do Not Track” signals.

11) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” above reflects the latest version.
