About SwiftPath Capital

SwiftPath Capital Corporation is a Tucson-based private lending and funding firm helping real estate investors and business owners secure business-purpose financing—across DSCR, fix-and-flip, and bridge loans. We are not a consumer personal loan provider or an owner-occupied mortgage lender.

Who We Are

Founded by James Lewis, SwiftPath Capital brings more than two decades of experience in real estate investing, mortgage origination, and business development. Our focus is simple: deliver capital with speed, integrity, and leadership.

Our Story

Since 2000, James has been involved in property acquisitions, mortgage origination, and private lending operations. SwiftPath Capital was built on leadership, problem-solving, and strong relationships with investors and funding partners. We operate with a fiduciary mindset—aligning loan products to client goals so our clients can win and scale.
