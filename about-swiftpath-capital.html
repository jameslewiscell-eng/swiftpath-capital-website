Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hard money loan for real estate investors?

A business‑purpose loan secured by investment property. SwiftPath Capital works with national lending partners to help investors fund purchases, rehab, and bridge financing quickly.

Do you fund owner‑occupied homes or consumer personal loans?

No. We only support business‑purpose loans for investment properties (DSCR, fix‑and‑flip, and bridge).

Which projects are a good fit for SwiftPath Capital?

Rental portfolios using DSCR, fix‑and‑flip projects, and short‑term bridge scenarios. We emphasize speed, reliable draws, and clear communication for investors.

Who are your lending partners?

Kiavi · RCN Capital · F Street · Lima One. We match programs to your goals.

How fast can I get pre‑qualified?

Most investors can get a quick pre‑qualification after submitting the application and completing SMS verification.
