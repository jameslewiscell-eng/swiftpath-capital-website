E‑Sign Consent

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

This E‑Sign Consent (“Consent”) allows SwiftPath Capital Corporation (“SwiftPath Capital,” “we,” “us,” “our”) and our lending partners to provide you with disclosures, notices, agreements, and other communications electronically (“Electronic Communications”). By proceeding, you agree to this Consent.

1) Scope of Consent

Your consent applies to all Electronic Communications related to your inquiries, applications, pre‑qualifications, authorizations, loan agreements, disclosures, adverse action notices, servicing, and any other documents we or our partners provide.

2) Delivery & Paper Copies

We will provide Electronic Communications by email, secure links, or through our websites/portals. You may request a free paper copy by emailing legal@swiftpathcapital.com. We may require reasonable time to fulfill requests.

3) Withdrawing Consent

You may withdraw your consent at any time by emailing legal@swiftpathcapital.com. Withdrawal will not affect the validity of Electronic Communications previously provided. If you withdraw consent, certain services or processes may be slower or unavailable.

4) Updating Your Contact Information

It is your responsibility to keep your email address and contact information current. Notify us of changes at legal@swiftpathcapital.com.

5) Hardware & Software Requirements

  • Device with internet access and a current web browser capable of TLS‑encrypted connections.
  • Ability to receive email and access PDF documents.
  • Current version of Adobe Acrobat Reader or similar to view/print PDFs.
  • Sufficient storage to save communications or a connected printer to print them.

6) Consent Confirmation

By checking the e‑sign consent checkbox in our application flow (or by otherwise indicating consent electronically), you confirm that you can access and retain Electronic Communications and that you consent to receive them electronically.
